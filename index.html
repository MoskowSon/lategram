How Lategram Works

Three steps. One beautiful experience.

Capture the Moment

Take a photo at any location. It's silently pinned to your GPS coordinates — anchored to that exact spot, waiting to be discovered.

Lategram Reads Your Contacts

That's it. No manual setup. When you arrive anywhere, Lategram checks your existing phone contacts against who's left memories there.

A Connection Surfaces

If a mutual contact — someone who also has you saved — was there before you, both of you are notified. Their photos unlock. The place comes alive.
